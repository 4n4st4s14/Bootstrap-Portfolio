About Me

 My greatest fear is that, on my death bed, I'll wish I'd spent more time on my computer. I am hoping this bootcamp will assuage that anxiety.

 My interest in programming was inspired by my passion for music, and time spent as an audio engineer. Both the software and hardware used in music production are evloving at an ever-increasing rate, and allow for nearly infinite customization - if you know how to program them. I am hoping this course will give me the practical skills needed to find gainful employment, while also giving me more insight on how to get the most out of my synths and sequencers.
